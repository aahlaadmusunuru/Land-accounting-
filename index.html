Introduction to land accounting

How QGIS has helped for the fiji National statistics officers ?

QGIS has helped us to visualize and also quantify the changes in land cover of a period of 11 years, which has aided us a lot in compiling our Experimental Land Cover Account for Fiji. We hope to be able to continue using this tool in the future as more up-to-date data becomes available. The feature that allows you to generate reports in csv format is quite neat as it allows you to then arrange and analyze the data whichever way you prefer.
